# Simulation Results Summary

## Converged Design

```text
regulated beta-energy source region
↓
diamond active region
↓
straight internal graphene or graphitic collector fingers
↓
metal contact routing
```

## Why This Design Survived

- Ferrite transport was eliminated as a poor bulk transport path.
- Embedded graphitic collectors improved collection by reducing carrier travel distance.
- Straight fingers survived manufacturing penalties better than complex meshes.
- Diamond remained the strongest substrate after material screening.
- Surface source placement remained preferable to embedded or distributed source layouts after packaging penalties.

## Current Hypothesis

A diamond radiovoltaic with internal straight graphitic or graphene collection fingers can improve charge collection by reducing carrier travel distance before recombination and trapping.

## Next Validation Step

Build a non-radioactive surrogate experiment to compare planar contacts against fingered collector geometries.
