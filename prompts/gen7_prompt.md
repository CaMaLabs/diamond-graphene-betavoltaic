# Gen 7 Prompt — Prototype Planning

Create a practical prototype and validation roadmap for a diamond and internal graphitic-collector radiovoltaic concept.

Evaluate:

1. Non-radioactive surrogate demonstrator.
2. Graphitic-channel diamond demonstrator.
3. Professional licensed-source validation path.

Required outputs:

- fabrication feasibility sequence,
- surrogate experiment plan,
- measurement protocol,
- safety and regulatory boundary,
- failure mode ranking,
- recommended first experiment,
- go/no-go criteria.

Optimize for the fastest safe experiment that can falsify or support the collector-geometry hypothesis.
