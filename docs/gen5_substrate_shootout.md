# Generation 5 — Substrate Shootout

## Question

Can another crystal substrate outperform diamond once cost and manufacturing constraints are included?

## Substrates Compared

- Diamond
- 4H-SiC
- 3C-SiC
- GaN
- Cubic boron nitride
- Aluminum nitride
- Beta gallium oxide
- Silicon
- Boron carbide
- GaAs

## Outcome

Diamond remained the best absolute performer in the relative screening model. 4H-SiC was the strongest non-diamond wide-bandgap challenger. Silicon won cost-adjusted rankings but not absolute performance.

## Design Lesson

Diamond appears difficult to replace because it combines radiation tolerance, thermal conductivity, wide bandgap behavior, and compatibility with internal graphitic collector concepts.
